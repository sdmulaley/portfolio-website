01. ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Scott, a 23 year old filmmaker based in Boston, MA

I have a BA in Communications and Media Studies from Dean College and have been working in video production for the past 10 years.

I sparked a passion for television and film production when I was in my freshman year of high school. I started work at SCTV (Sharon Cable Television), my local cable station. After a few months, I fell in love with television production. I started off by operating the studio cameras, which eventually led me to directing studio shows freely by my junior year.

My experience with SCTV has not only helped me discover my true passion, it led me into college feeling excited and prepared. Upon attending Dean College, I acquired many new skills and countless great opportunities. As a recent college graduate, I feel prepared and confident to take on whatever the world has to offer.
